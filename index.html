Competitions

Register to play and win!

Squad

A game mode where you can team up in groups of 2, 3 or 4 players and take on everyone in the match.

Duo

For this game mode, you will be paired up with another individual and will compete to be the last ones alive.

Solo

A game mode where you spawn into the world alone and you just rely on your own tactics and skill to push you to the end and be the last player alive.
